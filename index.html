ABOUT (ME)

In a few words, I promise.

Ideas Man

Matias Salinas

For the past two years since finishing school I have been smashing out a Bachelor of Multimedia Design at Bond University (I say smashing because we literally get no holidays). My majors being in Advertising and Communications. In my time I've inherited an unquenchable thirst for knowledge, which I then apply to come up with unique and creative solutions for any task I'm given. What I have really enjoyed from the course is being introduced to the challenging yet exciting world of advertising. I'm fascinated with the idea of cutting through the clutter, helping create something that resonates, is effective and unique. Which is why when I try to identify what I believe a 'good ad' to be. The first question I ask myself is 'Did it connect truthfully with the right target audience?' And 'Is the message valuable?' If no then it is just another ad.

From web design, to human comms to branding, I've had a taste for it all but what I am really hungry for is an opportunity for me to demonstrate my skills and abilities in this industry.

If you feel I might be the right fit your company or would simply like to know more about me, don't hesitate to get in touch.
